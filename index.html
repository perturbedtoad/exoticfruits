WHO WE ARE

Exotic Fruits is a government recognised export house from India manufacturing tropical trait purees and concentrates. Our products are used in several fruit-based industries including beverages, yoghurt, dairy and confectioneries.

At Exotic, quality always comes first, whether it be in product or in the service. Exotic is a participant in the Voluntary Control System of SGF, Germany and is an approved supplier under the IRMA programme.

The Exotic Edge

With the solid experience of many years in the industry, Exotic has an unbeatable edge to its offering. The reasons are evident:

  • Expertise of competent professionals with a rich experience of the fruit processing industry

  • Sophisticated processing plants to deliver only the best quality products

  • Processing plants located close to fruit growing areas minimize fruit handling and transportation

WHY US

Quality First

Exotic has established a detailed set of processes and steps to ensure that the end-product conforms to stringent international norms in the fruit processing industry. To this effect, all the processing plants have adequate capability for product testing with the help of sophisticated lab equipment. Parameters such as colour, brix, pH, viscosity (Bostwick as well as Brooketield) and microbial content are regularly checked for conformance to the established norms.

Accreditation

Exotic’s Ratnagiri and Krishnagiri plants have obtained an HACCP certification, in pursuance to its implementation of Good Manufacturing Practices. Other plants in Chittoor are also actively pursuing the HACCP certification. Also, the company is an approved supplier with the SGF, Germany under the IRMA programme. By participating in the Voluntary Control System of SGF, Exotic meets additional requirements for safeguarding the quality of its products and for increasing customer safety.
